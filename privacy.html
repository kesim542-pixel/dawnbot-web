1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how DawnBot ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and protects your personal information when you use our application and its TikTok integration features.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information exclusively to:

  • Authenticate your TikTok account
  • Post videos to your TikTok profile when you explicitly request it
  • Display your connected account name for confirmation

We do NOT: sell your data, share it with third parties, use it for advertising, or store it permanently.

4. TikTok API Data

DawnBot uses the following TikTok API scopes:

  • user.info.basic — To display your TikTok username after login
  • video.publish — To post videos directly to your TikTok profile
  • video.upload — To upload video content to TikTok servers

All data obtained through TikTok's API is handled in accordance with TikTok's Platform Policy and Developer Terms of Service.

5. Data Storage

DawnBot stores data temporarily in server memory only:

  • No personal data is written to permanent databases
  • All data is cleared when the server restarts
  • No cookies or tracking technologies are used

6. Data Sharing

We do not share your personal data with any third parties except:

  • TikTok — Required for core functionality (video posting)
  • Telegram — For bot interface functionality

7. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical measures to protect your data including:

  • HTTPS encryption for all data transmission
  • Access tokens are never logged or stored in plain text
  • Server access is restricted to authorized personnel only

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Revoke access: TikTok Settings → Privacy → Apps and Websites → DawnBot → Remove
  • Request deletion: Contact us to delete any stored data
  • Access information: Request details about what data we hold

9. Children's Privacy

DawnBot is not intended for users under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

10. Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify users of significant changes through our Telegram bot.

11. Contact Us

For privacy-related questions or concerns, contact us through our official Telegram channel.
