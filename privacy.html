Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 22, 2026

1. Information We Collect

When you connect your TikTok account to DawnBot, we collect:

• Your TikTok User ID (open_id)
• Your TikTok access token and refresh token
• Your TikTok display name (for confirmation purposes only)

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your TikTok access token solely to post videos to your TikTok profile when you explicitly request it through our Telegram bot. We do not use your data for any other purpose.

3. Data Storage

Access tokens are stored temporarily in memory only and are not persisted to any database or disk storage. Tokens are lost when the server restarts.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with any third parties except TikTok's API which is required for the core functionality of the service.

5. TikTok Data

Your use of TikTok features through DawnBot is subject to TikTok's own Privacy Policy available at https://www.tiktok.com/legal/privacy-policy

6. Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your information. However, no internet transmission is 100% secure.

7. Your Rights

You can revoke DawnBot's access to your TikTok account at any time by going to TikTok Settings → Apps and Services → DawnBot → Remove Access.

8. Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy at any time. We will notify users of significant changes through our Telegram bot.

9. Contact

For privacy-related questions, contact us through Telegram.
